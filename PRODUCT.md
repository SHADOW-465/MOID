# Product

## Register

product

## Users
The primary users of MO!D (RAIS Pro) are Pharma General Managers (GMs), Quality Engineers (QEs), and Plant Supervisors at regulated manufacturing operations (such as Disposafe). These users have high-stakes roles where they need to make rapid, auditable operational decisions under pressure. They require structured, clear, and mathematically precise information to check compliance with standards like ISO 13485 or CDSCO (MDR 2017).

## Product Purpose
MO!D provides an editorial diagnostic layer for regulated manufacturing plants. By converting messy plant spreadsheets and manual entries into clean, auditable dashboards and executive diagnostic reports, it helps GMs locate quality issues, identify catheter size anomalies, track SPC control limit violations, and review cost of poor quality (COPQ) in under 30 seconds.

## Brand Personality
- **Rigorous**: Scientific, precise, and highly detailed. Zero fluff or superficial animations.
- **Dignified**: Inspired by printed medical journals and executive dossiers. Features a warm paper canvas, near-black charcoal ink, and a burnt orange accent.
- **Restrained**: Relies on typography contrast, layout grids, and flat outlines instead of modern SaaS clichés.

## Anti-references
- **SaaS Clichés**: Neon purple/blue gradients, glowing background blobs, massive rounded corners (>24px).
- **Glassmorphism**: Backdrop blur cards, semi-transparent overlays that decrease contrast, and lack of visible borders.
- **Visual Gimmicks**: Emojis used as UI navigation icons, floating cards with no visible boundaries, and low-contrast grey text.

## Design Principles
1. **Editorial Authority**: Every screen should read like a page in an executive report. Spacing is intentional, and typography (Fraunces Display Serif + Inter Tight UI) establishes clear document structure.
2. **Traceable & Auditable**: Every metric shown can be traced directly back to its cell origin. The visual bezier ink beam is an interactive commitment to truth.
3. **Structured Restraint**: Visual hierarchy is maintained using standard borders, outline cards, and clean grid alignments. Decorative elements are disallowed.
4. **Resilient Color Contrast**: Text and visual highlights must satisfy high-contrast accessibility ratios (≥4.5:1 for body text) across both warm paper light mode and rich charcoal dark mode.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict compliance with WCAG AA contrast ratios.
- Full support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to tone down bezier traces and transition curves.
- Focus rings are explicitly drawn to guarantee clear keyboard navigation on the plant floor.
